BACKGROUND

I was born in Edinburgh and moved to Italy when I was 5. Ever since I have been going back and forth between the two countries. I attended school in Sicily and moved to Bologna to study music and cinema in Bologna. I have a keen interest in computers and art. I first started learning about computers when I was 10 and quickly started designing and developing websites. My interesest in art is mostly rooted in my love for music which started when I was 11 when I started playing guitar. Since then I have played music with hundreds of people and been in half a dozen bands. I am an avid learner and always have the urge to expand my knowledge and skills.
